Medals in International Science Olympiads

      Since 1991, many of our school students have won gold and silver medals in International Olympiads in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology and Informatics. In recent years, they have been qualified for the national team every year, earning our country great honors and our school a fine reputation as a ¡®gold medal school¡¯.

      Glory begins here in our school. Yet, more precious is the ¡®gold medal spirit¡¯, that is, the courage to achieve higher and better. The spirit has become a source of inspiration for countless teachers and students to obtain more knowledge and strive for the better.

Stars of Science and Technology

      As a demonstrative high school specialized in science and technology education, No. 2 High School of East China Normal University (ECNU) has gained rich experience over the years in training top talents of science and technology and a large number of students have won big prizes in international competitions in science and technology. Always keeping the idea of innovation in mind when it comes to the education of science and technology, the school does not just focus on students¡¯ accumulation of knowledge in science. It puts more emphasis on nurturing scientific way of thinking, scientific approaches and qualities in students as well as the spirit of science.
