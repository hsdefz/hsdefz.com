       As a base of national Hanban for international promotion of the Chinese language and a base of Shanghai Overseas Chinese Affairs Office for Chinese language education, our school has hosted many summer camps for 8 consecutive years, including Shanghai¡¯s International Sister Cities Youth Summer Camp organized by Shanghai Municipal Education Commission, Youth Summer Camp of ¡°Elite Chinese and Foreign Youth Forum¡± and Summer Camp of ¡°Searching for the Chinese Root¡± for Young Ethnic Chinese.

      High school students from Shanghai¡¯s Sister Cities were invited to join Shanghai¡¯s International Sister Cities Youth Summer Camp to learn Chinese, visit famous sites and experience culture in Shanghai. Foreign teenagers have got to know about Shanghai and China. For the 2013 camp, over 100 students from 16 countries came to our school.

      Our school has received over 1,000 teenagers from countries around the world including Canada, USA, UAE, Malaysia, Indonesia and the Philippines for Summer Camp of ¡°Searching for the Chinese Root¡± for Young Ethnic Chinese. They have been offered various Chinese language and culture programs, martial art programs and sightseeing in Shanghai.

      Our school also organizes Chinese language summer camps for learners from our sister schools. They receive intensive language programs, experience Chinese culture through daily life of a common Chinese family and visit famous sites in and around Shanghai.

      Our school is also in active preparation for summer camps for children of our alumni living abroad.
