Matriculations
Home   >   Academics   >   Matriculations    
          Every year, No. 2 High School of East China Normal University sends numerous outstanding graduates to first-class universities at home and abroad.  On average, over 99% of the total graduates enter universities successfully and over 95% go to key universities in China.  More importantly, apart from the fact that the number of our graduates admitted into top universities in China is huge, they also develop very well at university and receive good comments not only from their universities and the whole society, but also from other students and parents.  In recent years, more and more of our graduates have been applying for overseas universities and they mostly go to top universities in USA, Canada and Japan.  Over 40 graduates have been enrolled each year into Yale, Stanford, MIT, the University of Chicago, Cornell University, UC Berkeley in the US, University of Toronto, University of Victoria in Canada, and Osaka University, Tohoku University in Japan in the past two years.
