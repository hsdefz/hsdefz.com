          Established in 1958, No.2 High School Attached to ECNU was appointed as one of the high schools under the direct supervision of Shanghai Education Bureau and was further confirmed in 1978 as the only high school that was under the direct supervision of China¡¯s Ministry of Education. In 2005, it was awarded as one of the first batch of Model Experimental Senior High Schools in Shanghai, as well as in 2007, a National Advanced Team Member in Education System by the Ministry of Personnel and Ministry of Education. The school is also a Model Unit in Shanghai, a National Modern Education Technique Experimental School, a ¡°2049¡± Innovative Talent Base, a Science and Technology Featured School in Shanghai, a member of UNESCO APEID as well.

        In 1985, the school was relocated to the new campus at Zaoyang Road, and subsequently in 2002, it was relocated to the current site in Zhangjiang High-Tech Park, Pudong New Area. The brand new campus features various top-rated teaching facilities with a total investment of 300 million RMB, a site area of about 25 acres with 60000 square meters¡¯ construction area. In 1999, the International Division was set up. Currently the school has 1,500 students both from home and abroad and 118 full-time teachers among whom 11 are top-rated teachers.

        Following the requirement from the Ministry of Education that key high school shall actively promote the educational reform to accumulate experience and grow their own talents pool, the school has established the principle of ¡°Quality stands, technology thrives, and innovation grows¡±. Ever since the end of the 1980¡¯s, the school has gradually carried out an in-depth education reform involving comprehensive aspects holding an aim of ¡°Pursuing Excellence and Nurturing the Future Creator¡± focusing on talents that are ¡°excellent & with specialties¡±. With the school¡¯s initiation to set up ¡°Leading Teacher System¡± nationwide, and gradually forming the ¡°Six 100%¡± quality education model, we have achieved the goal of creating a Gold Medal School for Moral Education. Furthermore, based on rich experiences accumulated through the years, a curriculum with international competitive level has been created, which not only ensures the further enhancement of the students¡¯ overall development, but also accelerates the development of the specialties and capabilities of our teachers.

        Always keeping ahead in educational teaching level in the process of educational reform in China, No.2 High School Attached to ECNU has witnessed surging groups of talented graduates that testify the fruitful achievements in the school¡¯s all-round education development system.
