Peddie School in New Jersey, US

      Founded in 1864, Peddie School is a renowned college preparatory school in Hightstown, New Jersey, United States with a long history. It is a coeducationalboarding school serving students in the ninth through twelfth grades. Located on a 230-acrecampus and surrounded by lakes and woods, it is well known for the beautiful environment. Accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools Commission, it enjoys a fine reputation as one of the best boarding schools in the US, nurturing countless talented students over the years. The school has 530 students from 23 states and 23 countries. A member of the New Jersey Independent School Athletic Association (NJISAA), Peddie has well integrated innovation into tradition in terms of students¡¯ academic study, sports training and arts development. With the advancement of China¡¯s economy, increase of Sino-US communication and establishment of sister-school relationship between Peddie and our school, the demand of learning Chinese language from Peddie students and communities around has been on the rise. There is also a steady increase in the number of Chinese language teachers in need. The future of Chinese language education in Peddie and around is certainly very bright.

School website: www.peddie.org

 

Lake Forest High School

      Founded in 1935, Lake Forest High School is a renowned public school located in Lake Forest City, north west of Chicago. It has about 2,000 students from grades 9 to 12. The school not only has complete and advanced facilities but also enjoys a strong faculty of high-quality teachers with over 95% holding a master¡¯ degree or a higher one. As one of the best high schools in Chicago, Lake Forest implements an idea of overall education of a person, focusing on academic achievement as well as psychological health growth.

School website: www.lfhs.org

 

La Jolla Country Day School

      La Jolla Country Day School, known informally as "LJCDS", is a highly selective coed private school, once voted the best in San Diego. It has over 1,100 students from grades 3 to 12. Founded in 1926, it has been devoting itself to nurturing young citizens with a high sense of responsibility and creativity through strict academic training and educational environment.
      The school has advanced facilities in teaching, arts and sports not to mention its strong faculty. It has 6 tennis courts, a weight-lifting room and various sports venues of baseball, softball, football, and soccer. It also has a center of visual arts and science.
      Country Day students have performed very well in SAT and AP tests over the years.

School website: www.ljcds.org
